ADI SHANKARA INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

Adi Shankara Institute of Engineering & Technology (ASIET) is ideally situated in a picturesque environment that evokes vivid memories of Jagadguru Adi Shankara's calm presence. It is affiliated to the A P J Abdul Kalam Technological University, approved by the AICTE and offers courses in UG, PG and PhD levels.
